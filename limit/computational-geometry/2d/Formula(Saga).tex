\subsubsection{Pick's Theorem}
\begin{eqnarray*}
S=I+\frac{B}{2}-1
\end{eqnarray*}
$S$ is the area of lattice polygon, $I$ is the number of lattice interior points, and $B$ is the number of lattice boundary points.
